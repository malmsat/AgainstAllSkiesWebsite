Bring Revolution to the Skies.

Navigate treacherous sky islands, build unique decks for a crew of unlikely heroes and breach the storm to uncover the true cost of power.

About the Game

Choose your own path through the skies as you fly your royal airship, the Helmholtz, between different islands, each with their own unique traits and environments. These biomes can help or hinder your plans to reach the eye of the storm. With procedurally generated nodes and a new formation each run, experience an endlessly replayable sequence of sky islands.

Features

  • Assemble up to 10 different combinations of characters for your party
  • Enjoy endlessly different deckbuilding approaches and experiment with hundreds of cards
  • Dive into a dynamic turn-based combat system leading to unexpected advantages and combos when Resonating with your party
  • Navigate through different island environments that bring their own challenges and rewards each run
  • Unlock party-enhancing artifacts or character-specific gadgets
  • Experience a rich world and cast of characters with their own personal journeys
