About the Game

Choose your own path through the skies as you fly your royal airship, the Helmholtz, between different islands, each with their own unique traits and environments. With procedurally generated nodes and a new formation each run, experience an endlessly replayable sequence of sky islands.

In Against All Skies, you can advance or delay party members during combat to achieve Resonance turns. Empowered by this teamwork, characters gain more tactical variety and activate enhanced abilities.

Experiment with new combos to unlock the full potential of your party as they venture into the unknown.

Features

  • Assemble up to 10 different combinations of characters for your party
  • Enjoy endlessly different deckbuilding approaches and experiment with hundreds of cards
  • Dive into a dynamic turn-based combat system leading to unexpected advantages and combos when Resonating with your party
  • Navigate through different island environments that bring their own challenges and rewards each run
  • Unlock party-enhancing artifacts or character-specific gadgets
  • Experience a rich world and cast of characters with their own personal journeys
